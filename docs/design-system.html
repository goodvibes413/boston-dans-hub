Design Principles

Hierarchy Through Color
Primary green is the only bright color. Use it sparingly for actions and highlights. Everything else is dark and muted.
Dark Theme
All surfaces are dark; text is light. High contrast ensures readability and reduces eye strain.
Consistency
Every interactive element uses the same button/card treatment. No one-offs. Predictable, trustworthy interactions.
Density
Tight spacing on mobile, generous on desktop. Respect screen real estate constraints while maintaining breathing room.
Boston Aesthetic
The green echoes Boston sports heritage. Paired with dark backgrounds, it feels modern, bold, and distinctly Boston.
Accessibility
High contrast text, clear hierarchy, and intuitive interactions ensure everyone can use and enjoy Boston Dan's Hub.
